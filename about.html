Vidya Mandir is one of the most reputed, a co-educational, English medium school guided by Sita Kunwar Education and development Trust, Dandibag, Gaya. It is situated on the bank of holy river Falgu at Dandibag Gaya in pollution free, tranquil and natural environment. So many students participate each and every year in different competetions and take honorable position. Vidya Mandir is not in the need of any introduction. It is the petal of the flower of Indian Cuulture. Motivation and inspiration are the methods of our glowing teaching.

Our Mission

Our mission is to develop young men with active and creative minds, a sense of understanding and compassion for others and the courage to act on their beliefs.

We stress the total development of each child : spritual, moral intellectual, social, emotional and physical to make a good citizen of the country.

Our mission is to educate students and help them appreciate the better points of the knowledge that's being imparted to them.
Our mission is to ensure that good and positive sensitivities are inculcated in the student's mind.

Our Our mission is to provide a unique and innovative world class learning environment for each student's holistic development with Indian ethos and global standards.

Our Our mission is to see that every child is sculpted as World Citizen, ready to go a path of high Caliber professionalism and good human.

Our Our mission is to evolve deep respect and commitment for the culture of India & respect towards other.

Our Our mission is to make the learning processes enjoyable and satisfying by using innovative methods.
