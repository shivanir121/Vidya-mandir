ADMISSIONS


ADMISSIONS OPEN FOR 2020
Admission will be alloted for the std L.K.G, U.K.G (CBSE) AND for std. LKG to VIth (BSEB).
Admission test will be held on Wednesssday 12/02/2020 at 8:30 AM.
The seats are not available for std 1st to VIth (CBSE).
Candidate has to bring their original passport size photo.

Instructions for new enrollment
1. Get the receipt and admit card for the test enrollment.
2. The present photo and signature of candidate and also signature of parents/guardian is required in the Admit card.
3. After checking the model question paper, make sure that for which class the test should be taken.
4. The examination will be held for three subjects Hindi, English and Mathematics. It is mandatory to attain all the three subjects.
5. It is mandatory to score at least 40% marks in each subject.
6. After result declaration, it is mandatory to meet principal and mark the class on the receipt.
7. To get the admission form from the office at the time of enrollment, submit a photo and the prescribed amount along with the receipt and admit card.
8. A signature with the current photo of the candidate and his / her parents is required during enrollment. The parents who live outside can make responsible to any person (guardian). For this, his/her current photo and signature is mandatory and he/she has to sign in front of office member.
9. After enrollment, get the receipt of the deposit amount.
10. It is required to take admission within 7 days. After that admission session will be closed.

School rules
The parents and the students must very well understand the rules and strictly abide by them to the desired extent.

School expects from its students to follow the rules and regulations as furnished time to time.

students are expected to ever remain obedient and devoted to their teachers so as to ensure a very healthy, positive and rewarding academic atmosphere.

Students must attend the school only in prescribed uniform on all class days. A student who is slovenly dressed may be exclussed from the classroom or even sent home.

No student is allowed to keep with himself or herselfthings like money in cash. Calculator, necklace, ring and other costly things.

Indiscipline, Indifference to studies, irregularity in attendence, obscenity in words or any other misconduct even outside the school. Premises are valid reasons for the expulsion of a student.

Keeping school and classes neat and clean is student's responsibility.
Serious punishment will be applied after damaging anything of school property by any student.

Writting on walls of school is strictly prohibited.

The school will not be questionable for any action if takes against any student in case of any rule or regulation is violated.

Students are themselves responsible for the article that may vring to school.

Parents-teachers meeting are organised in the school at regular intervals. information regarding these meetings are sent to parents thorough their words. parents are requested to attend these meetings.

On special occasion like marriage, maximum five days and on death (bhoj) four days leave will be granted duly signed by guardian.

Economical punishment will be charged in case of asbsence of the students more than two days a month.

School fees must be deposited in time falling which may prove emotionally disturbing for the students.

School fees will be accepted without late fine from the end of the month to 15th of the next month otherwise Rs 20/- will be charged as late fine from 16th to 25th and Rs 40/- will be charged as late fine from 26th to the end of the month.

Name of the student will be deleted from attendance register if the school fee will not be deposited till the fixed time.
